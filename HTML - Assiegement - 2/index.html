Recipe: Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients:

  1. Buns
    • Sesame seed hamburger buns

  2. Patties
    • Pepper
    • Salt
    • Ground beef

  3. Toppings
    • Cheese
      • Cheddar
      • Swiss
    • Lettuce
    • Onion rings
    • Tomato slices
    • Pickles

  4. Condiments
    • Mayonnaise
    • Mustard
    • Ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Patties

    • In a bowl, combine ground beef with salt and pepper.
    • Form the mixture into equal-sized patties.

  2. Cook the Patties

    • Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat
    • Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until desired doneness.
    • During the last minute of cooking, place cheese slices on top of the patties to melt.

  3. Toast the Buns

    • Lightly butter the insides of the buns.
    • Toast them on the grill or skillet until golden brown.

  4. Assemble the Burger

    • Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun.
    • Add desired toppings:
      • Pickles
      • Onion rings
      • Tomato slices
      • Tomato
    • Spread condiments on the top bun:
      • Mayonnaise
      • Mustard
      • Ketchup
    • Place the top bun over the assembled ingredients.
